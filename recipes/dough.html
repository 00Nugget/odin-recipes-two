Two Ingredient Dough



Impress with this elegant and simple dough recipe!


Prep time: 15 minutes
Servings : 4

Ingredients

  • 11/4 cups self-rising four (whole wheat preferred)
  • 1 cup low-fat plain Greek yogurt

Directions

Step 1.A

Empty flour in a bowl. add yogurt and stir until dough forms. Knead on a lightly floured surface until the dough is smooth. Add water until texture is sticky

Step 1.B

Alternatively,place dough in a mixer. Add yogurt and water as necessary. Texture should be sticky

Tip

Don't over knead the dough. Give it a light smack at the end for good luck!
