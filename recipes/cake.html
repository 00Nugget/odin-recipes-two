Scrumptious Pumpkin Cake



This pumpkin cake is both easy to make and delicious. With only two ingredients, this can be a great addition to any last minute dinner party!


Prep time: 5 minutes
Serves 15
Cook time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 package of spice cake mix
  • 1 can of pumpkin

Directions

Step 1
Gather ingredients.
Step 2
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees Celsius)
Step 3
Grease baking dish with cooking spray
Step 4
Mix ingredients in a large bowel until well combined; spread evenly into baking dish
Step 5
Bake in oven for 25 - 30 minutes. Stick a toothpick or thin utensil in the center of the cake. If it comes out clean, your cake is ready to come out of the oven!
Step 6
Cool and Serve!
